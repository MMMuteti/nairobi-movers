We move Nairobi

We’re a local team of movers and coordinators who believe moving should be straightforward, safe and predictable. Since 2019 we’ve completed thousands of moves across Kenya.

  • In-house trained crews
  • Modern, well-maintained trucks
  • Insured services available

Our process

  1. Survey: Virtual or on-site assessment & inventory.
  2. Quote: Clear, itemised proposal with options.
  3. Pack: Protective packing & labelling.
  4. Move: Careful loading, transport & unloading.
  5. Setup: Reassembly & placement at destination.
